Photorealistic CGI Visualisation

I design interiors and bespoke furniture, creating unique and functional spaces. My studio is where clients’ visions become reality — from concept through to final execution. I specialize in interior design and bespoke furniture, with a strong focus on detail, quality, and craftsmanship. Alongside design, I produce high-end visualisations that communicate atmosphere, materiality, and spatial intent with clarity. My work is informed by years of experience working with London-based studios, combining British design sensibility with a solid technical background in cabinet making and production. This allows me to move seamlessly from concept to precise, workshop-ready drawings. I also develop photorealistic visualisations for design studios, joinery workshops, and private clients — from early concepts to fully resolved interiors. Projects can be based on a range of inputs, including mood boards, reference imagery, or existing spaces, translating initial ideas into clear and realistic visual outcomes

About

In my studio, I specialise in interior design and crafting bespoke luxury furniture. I honed my furniture design skills in the heart of London’s design scene – in Chelsea and Fulham – while technical drawing and craftsmanship were passed down through my family’s woodworking tradition spanning three generations.
